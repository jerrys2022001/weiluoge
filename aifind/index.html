Frequently asked questions

Can Find AI help me find lost AirPods?

Yes. Find AI scans nearby Bluetooth signals and shows a live distance radar to guide you toward your lost AirPods or earbuds.

Does Find AI work with non-Apple earbuds or headphones?

Yes. Find AI works with many Bluetooth devices such as earbuds, headphones, and Beats-compatible accessories when they are nearby and discoverable.

What happens when my device is out of range?

Find AI stores last seen time and location on device, so you can return to the last detected area and continue scanning.

Is Find AI private and secure?

Find AI processes Bluetooth finder data on device. According to the privacy policy, device-finding data is not uploaded for analytics or model training.
