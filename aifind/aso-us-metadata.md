## App Store Description (problem -> capability -> proof -> CTA)

Lose less time when devices go missing.

Find AI is a bluetooth finder app built to help you find lost AirPods, earbuds, Beats, and other nearby Bluetooth devices fast.

What makes Find AI practical:
- Real-time distance radar: convert signal strength into near/far guidance while you move.
- Smart device grouping: separate known devices from unknown noise.
- Pin and prioritize: keep important devices ready at the top.
- Last seen memory: recover from out-of-range moments with on-device last seen time and map clue.

Built for everyday recovery:
- Lost AirPods at home
- Earbuds misplaced at work
- Headphones left behind while traveling

Privacy by design:
- Device-finding data is processed on device.
- See full details: https://velocai.net/aifind/privacy-policy.html

Download Find AI and start recovering nearby devices in minutes.
