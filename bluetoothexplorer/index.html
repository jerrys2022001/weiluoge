Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from developers and test teams evaluating a BLE scanner and GATT terminal on iOS.

What is Bluetooth Explorer used for?

Bluetooth Explorer is an iOS BLE debugging app for scanning devices, checking GATT services, sending commands, reviewing logs, and speeding up troubleshooting with AI insights.

Does Bluetooth Explorer support hex terminal commands?

Yes. The app supports hex, text, binary, and decimal modes so you can test packets in the format your firmware uses.

Can I inspect GATT services and characteristics?

Yes. After connecting, you can browse discovered services and characteristics, then open read or write flows for detailed BLE verification.

How does the app help with BLE troubleshooting?

Bluetooth Explorer records timeline logs, lets you export session evidence, and uses AI diagnostics to highlight possible protocol or connection issues.

Does Bluetooth Explorer process debug data in the cloud?

According to the app privacy policy, BLE debugging and device-finding related diagnostics are processed on-device and not uploaded for analytics or model training.
